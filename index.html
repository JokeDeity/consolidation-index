Global Control Index

The world is currently undergoing a transition away from independent nations toward a system where a small group of superstates hold all primary global power.

This index tracks the merger of independent territories into these dominant hegemonic blocks. 100% represents the threshold of total unified control, where no nation can maintain military or economic sovereignty without the permission of a superstate core.

Methodology & Development

This index is an ongoing monitoring system currently under development. The percentage is calculated using a non-linear scale that weighs kinetic military presence, the capture of regional trade ledgers, and the dissolution of local administrative borders. Because the resistance of remaining sovereign states increases as control tightens, movements above 80% are modeled to be significantly more difficult to achieve, ensuring the data reflects real-world friction rather than linear projection.
